The classifications of algebraic varieties and their moduli spaces Seminars

Organizers: Zhengyu Hu (Chongqing), Sichen Li (Shanghai), Haidong Liu (Guangzhou) and Shizhuo Zhang (Bonn).

The purpose of this seminar is to introduce recent results the classifications of algebraic varieties and their moduli spaces.

If you are interested in giving a talk on your results, please contact the organizers： Zhengyu Hu (zhengyuhu16@gmail.com), Sichen Li (sichenli@ecust.edu.cn), Haidong Liu (liuhd35@mail.sysu.edu.cn) and Shizhuo Zhang (shizhuozhang@mpim-bonn.mpg.de).
